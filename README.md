# facebookimageshare
Facebook Image Share

Swift 2.2, XCode 7.3, ios 9.3

A sample app that posts image as a custom story on facebook, by uploading the image to imgur through anonymous upload option.
App is for tutoring purposes (part of a blog post on brotherlemon.com) and won't be updated with future releases of either facebook or imgur APIs, or new versions of iOS, XCode, Swift.
